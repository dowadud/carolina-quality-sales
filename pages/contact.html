Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and processes

What are your business hours?

We're open Monday through Friday from 8AM to 6PM, and Saturday from 9AM to 4PM. We're closed on Sundays and major holidays.

How quickly can you respond to inventory requests?

We typically respond to inventory inquiries within 2-4 hours during business hours. For urgent requests, please call us directly.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we work with several financing partners to help dealers with their wholesale purchases. Contact us to learn more about available options.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve dealers throughout North and South Carolina, but we can arrange transportation to other southeastern states.

How do you ensure vehicle quality?

Every vehicle undergoes a comprehensive inspection process and comes with a detailed condition report. We only stock vehicles that meet our high-quality standards.

Can I visit your facility?

Yes, we welcome dealer visits by appointment. Please call ahead to schedule a time to view our inventory and facility.
