About Carolina Quality Sales

15+ years of excellence in automotive wholesale solutions

Our Story

Founded in 2009, Carolina Quality Sales began with a simple mission: to provide automotive dealers across the Carolinas with access to premium wholesale vehicles and exceptional service. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the region's most trusted automotive wholesale partners.

Our founder, experienced in both retail and wholesale automotive markets, recognized the need for a more transparent, reliable wholesale partner. Today, we serve over 200 dealer partners and move more than 500 vehicles monthly, maintaining the same commitment to quality and integrity that founded our company.

We believe that successful partnerships are built on trust, quality, and mutual respect. Every vehicle in our inventory is carefully selected and thoroughly inspected to meet our rigorous standards, ensuring our dealer partners receive only the best vehicles for their lots.
